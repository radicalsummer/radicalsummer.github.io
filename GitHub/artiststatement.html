ARTIST STATEMENT

My name is Summer Jones, and I am a studio practice with prep for teaching major at San Jose State. I focus mostly on self idenity, sexism, my Catholic culture, drug education, feminism, and many more topics within my artwork. Photography, painting, sculpture are my strongest skills, and I have just begun to learn some digital media. My overall goal as an artist is to be able to spread valuable knowledge, empathy, bring people together, and always help them look their best.
